About

Neatly folded away in Tribeca, a modest storefront joins the creative minds of Japan to the trendsetters, standouts, and free spirits of New York City. At Ambassador-NYC, we are lovers of the original and the daring. Our treasured designers will bring to you a world of uncharted creativity and rare charm. As curators of the most distinct and exclusive Japanese styles, we are committed to delivering designs that set our patrons apart from the crowd. Along with delivering unique styles and products, we endeavor to deepen the relationship between the designers we promote and the community we serve beyond a common trade of goods. With this commitment in mind, we have created a designer residency program. Through this effort, we host designers as tenants for three to twelve months and allow them a chance to connect with the city where their work will be made available. With this opportunity we hope our designers can discover new inspiration in beautiful New York City and our customers can seize a chance to interact with these phenomenal creative minds at our store. Here at Ambassador, it is our job to guide you beyond the horizon of convention and connect you with Japanese designers who are adventurously engaged with the extraordinary.
